About Us

The COVID-19 pandemic has proven the importance of clear, concise, and accurate communication and knowledge translation. News media outlets have been clouding the accuracy of information regarding the COVID-19 pandemic that is distributed to the public. In turn, this has caused the general public further confusion and increased misunderstanding of all things COVID-19 related. This application is meant to provide you with access to accurate, scientific journal publication resources and Canadian wide COVID statistics in comparison to news publications. We hope that this application will appropriately inform you amidst the misinformation created by the news and media.
